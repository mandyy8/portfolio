Why help others?

Growing up as a first generation Asian-American immigrant with only Chinese speaking parents taught me the important of independence at a young age. I had to learn how to do many things on my own.

So, when people helped me while expecting nothing back in my most difficult times, I felt supported and like I could take on the whole world. Life can be pretty short, but a legacy lasts forever. In order to pass it forward, my goal is to help other's find their meaning and make change, so my knowledge lives on beyond what I've personally done.

" Legacy is not what I did for myself. It's what I'm doing for the next generation. " - Vitor Belfort
